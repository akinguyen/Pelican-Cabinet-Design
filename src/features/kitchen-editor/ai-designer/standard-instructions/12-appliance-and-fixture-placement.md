# 12 — Appliance and Fixture Placement

Use catalog appliances and fixtures only when available. Do not invent appliances or fixtures.

## Cooking appliances

- Center a hood over the range or cooktop when both are used.
- Provide landing/prep space on both sides when possible.
- Avoid placing a range directly tight against a tall pantry/fridge if another valid arrangement exists.

## Sink and faucet

- Place sink and faucet as a coordinated cleanup zone.
- If a window/opening exists and the user wants a traditional sink placement, center the sink under the window when possible and valid.
- Place dishwasher directly beside the sink when a dishwasher catalog definition is available.

## Countertop-hosted appliances/fixtures

Drop-in sinks and cooktops should align with the countertop they cut. The engine derives `DerivedCountertopOpening`; do not output countertop opening data.

## Wall openings

Door/window objects must be placed on allowed wall segment faces. The engine derives `DerivedWallOpening`; do not output wall opening data.
