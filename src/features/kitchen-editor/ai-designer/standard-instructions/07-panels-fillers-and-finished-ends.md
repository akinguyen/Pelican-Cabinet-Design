# 07 — Panels, Fillers, and Finished Ends

Panels and fillers solve different construction conditions.

## Fillers

Use fillers to close intentional gaps:

- cabinet to wall
- cabinet to corner
- cabinet to appliance
- blind cabinet pull/clearance area when needed
- small remaining spaces that cannot be solved with standard cabinet widths

Fillers are not decorative exposed side panels.

## Finished panels

Use panels to finish exposed cabinet sides facing open air, especially at the end of a run, around tall cabinets, refrigerator panels, islands/peninsulas, or appliance towers.

## Do not double count

Do not use a filler and a finished panel for the same exact exposed end condition. Choose the object that matches the real construction condition.

## AI placement

Panels and fillers are still placed assemblies and must obey wall face permission when wall-based. Use catalog dimensions and valid option values.
